# Steel Beam Estimation Automation

Objective:
Read DXF files and estimate beam steel quantity in KG and MT.

Inputs:
1. General Notes DXF
2. Beam Framing + Reinforcement DXF

Outputs:
1. Beam-wise Steel Quantity
2. Diameter-wise Steel Quantity (Dia)
3. Total Steel KG
4. Total Steel MT

Business Rules:
- Extract Development Length from General Notes
- Extract Cover from General Notes
- Extract Beam Geometry from Framing Plan
- Extract Reinforcement from GFC Drawings
- Calculate Cutting Length
- Calculate Steel Weight
